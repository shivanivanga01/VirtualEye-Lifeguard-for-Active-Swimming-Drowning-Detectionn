About Underwater Safety System


Swimming is one of the best exercises that helps people reduce stress in urban lifestyles. However, drowning remains a significant risk, especially for children under six, who suffer the highest mortality rates worldwide. To combat this issue, we propose an innovative underwater safety system that utilizes AI to monitor swimmers' movements and detect potential drowning incidents.


By employing a single underwater camera connected to an AI system, we can analyze swimmer positions and assess the probability of drowning. If the risk exceeds a certain threshold, an alert will be generated to notify lifeguards. This approach aims to enhance safety in swimming pools, particularly in hotels and tourist spots, where drowning incidents are prevalent.
